Hi. I'm a graduate student in the lab of Tim Gardner at Boston University's Graduate Program in Neuroscience

In the lab, I'm developing calcium imaging in awake behaving songbirds. This webpage details some of the experiments and projects than I'm working on. Broadly, my intrests include: microscopy, memory coding, biological basis of computation, open-source science, building things, trying to program, eating good food and drinking bad coffee.

Population Recording

Neural Engineering
